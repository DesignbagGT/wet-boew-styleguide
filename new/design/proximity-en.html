Reflow content around a grid cell

The mrgn-*-md creates margin of 15px. Although grids use padding instead of margins, the gutter of a grid is also 15px. This is important to understand when mixing grids and non-grids in the same row. In order for the content will reflow around the grid cell, yet have the gutters match, you must apply both the mrgn-lft-md and mrgn-rght-md to the non-grid content.
